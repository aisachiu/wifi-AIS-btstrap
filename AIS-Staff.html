Compatibility

Older devices may not be compatible.

Initial testing shows that the older 2.4GHz wifi frequency is unstable. This is due to all that interference from other wifi, personal hotspots, and noise.

You'll need a
5'er

To access “AIS Staff”, your device will need to be able to connect to a 5GHz network. 802.11ac devices are preferable and will perform better.



Your device should save a certificate / login details so that you do not need to enter them again.

You may need admin privileges to trust and save the certificate to your device.

I Can't Find "AIS Staff"

If you can’t find the AIS Staff network on your device, it is likely that your device doesn’t support 5GHz wifi. Time for a new phone. Or maybe you are off campus (the wifi is only available on AIS campus). Or maybe you need to turn your wifi on?
